about me

Heyo! Welcome to my portfolio.


My name is Caio Teixeira, I am a content manager/creator with many years of experience shifting roles to Full Stack Development. Began my career as a journalist specialized in video games in 2006 as a freelancer. In late 2007 promoted to Editor in Chief of Arena Turbo, the video games branch of iG - one of the first news portals in Brazil. In 2010 became Executive Editor, being responsible for managing the editorial team, content planning and advertisement strategy.


In 2016 I was entrusted with the Esports Editor for Riot Games, assuming the editorial strategy, team management and public and internal communications. With the expansion of Riot's games catalogue, in late 2019 I saw an opportunity of stepping up my responsibilities and became Content Team Lead for creating, planning and aligning whole content campaigns for all the company's titles (League of Legends, VALORANT, Wild Rift and Legends of Runeterra).


In 2021 I started studying software development looking for using my comms abilities in conjunction with my newfound passion for tech.
